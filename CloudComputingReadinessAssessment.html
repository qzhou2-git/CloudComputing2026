Next-Step Reflection

Reflection Guide

How to use your results: Think about your confidence, prior coursework, hands-on experience, and career goals. The assessment is intended to support informed conversations, not make a decision for you.

Higher Readiness

You may already have a strong foundation. Consider whether you want extra AWS hands-on practice or prefer to focus on upper-level BAT coursework.

Developing Readiness

You may understand many concepts but benefit from targeted review in selected areas such as networking, security, or AWS core services.

Emerging Readiness

You may want to explore additional learning resources, tutorials, or optional coursework to build confidence before advanced cloud courses.

Questions to Consider

  • Do I feel comfortable explaining core cloud concepts without looking them up?
  • Have I completed hands-on labs using AWS or another cloud platform?
  • Do I understand cloud security responsibilities, networking basics, and cost awareness?
  • Would optional review help me feel more confident in upper-level BAT cloud courses?
  • Should I discuss my results with a faculty advisor or program contact?
